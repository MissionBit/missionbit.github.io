November 14, 2019, 6pm-9pm, San Francisco

Mission Bit's Third Annual Gala is a celebration of six years of growth, impact, and learning. Due to the support of this generous community, we have reached over 3,500 students through our workshops, coding courses, and career prep classes. We're on pace to reach 10,000 students by the year 2020, and we plan to do that together with you. Join us for this inspiring event, meet our students, hear their stories, and help us reach our 2020 goal. For more information on sponsorships, please contact us at development@missionbit.com.

With your help, we will achieve our goal of serving 10,000 students a year by the year 2020.
