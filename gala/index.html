Save the Date Add to Calendar

November 14, 2019, 6pm-9pm, San Francisco

Mission Bit's Third Annual Gala is a celebration of six years of growth, impact, and learning. Due to the support of this generous community, we have reached over 3,500 students through our workshops, coding courses, and career prep classes. We're on pace to reach 10,000 students by the year 2020, and we plan to do that together with you. Join us for this inspiring event, meet our students, hear their stories, and help us reach our 2020 goal.

For more information on sponsorships, see our Gala Sponsorship Packages or contact us at development@missionbit.com.

Dress: Dressy Casual. Please refer to the following Attire Guide for examples of Gala wear.

Parking: Street Parking + Parking Garage @ 1 Embarcadero Center

Location: The Bently Reserve is located on the corner of 301 Battery St at Sacramento St (View Map)

What to Expect:

  • Booths featuring amazing student projects
  • Open bar with specialty cocktails
  • Delicious San Francisco-inspired food
  • Great network of folks to connect with who care about our young people

We're excited to share this very special evening with you!
