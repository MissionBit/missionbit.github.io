Sponsor the 2019 Mission Bit Gala

November 14, 2019, 6pm-9pm, San Francisco

Mission Bit's Third Annual Gala is a celebration of six years of growth, impact, and learning. Due to the support of this generous community, we have reached over 3,500 students through our workshops, coding courses, and career prep classes. We're on pace to reach 10,000 students by the year 2020, and we plan to do that together with you. Join us for this inspiring event, meet our students, hear their stories, and help us reach our 2020 goal.

Check out our 2018 Annual Report for more about our vision and current impact. If you have any questions, please contact us at development@missionbit.com.

Mission Bit is a 501 (c)(3) public charity that strives to eliminate the tech divide for youth living in urban poverty and rural areas across the San Francisco Bay Area by building computer programming and professional opportunity pathways for public schools students. For more information, contact us at development@missionbit.com.
