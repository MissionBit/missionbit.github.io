A hacker space for public high school students to explore, learn and build with volunteer engineers and entreprenuers

The Hacker Lab

@ Mission High School, Room 404

In Q1 2015, the Hacker Lab will be open:
-> Monday & Wednesday from 3:30pm - 6pm
-> Tuesday & Thursday from 2:30pm - 6pm

The Hacker Lab is a pillar program of Mission Bit's site-based computer programming pathway model.

The Hacker Lab is a creative, relaxed, fun and highly supportive entry point for SF public high school students to begin to learn to code and to explore what they can build and accomplish writing code.

The Hacker Lab is a an accelerant for active SF public high school coders.

Help us equip the Hacker Lab with cool tech and hire high school students as 'lab assistants'!

Support The Hacker Lab

We are looking for mentors to volunteer to work any and all SFUSD high school students building stuff in the lab from 4pm - 6pm Monday - Thursday. Volunteer an hour per week or an hour per month.

Mentor in the Hacker Lab
