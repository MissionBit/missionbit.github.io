Free Coding Classes for Public School Students

2014 Mobile Game Development Demo Day

Description

Twelve SFUSD (San Francisco Unified School District) public high school students from Mission Bit's free summer 2014 immersive mobile game development class presented the 'shippable' games they'd built working together in teams 5 days/week for 7 hours/day since June 2014.This was a day when a panel of game industry and mobile app professionals provided feedback to the class game teams and all Demo Day attendees had the opportunity to play the games and meet the students.

Demo Day was an excellent chance for attendees to meet the rising crop of San Francisco native rock star game developers and get an update on Mission Bit's plans for the 2014/2015 school year.

The summer 2014 mobile game development class was sponsored by WeChat and supported by co.lab, The Salesforce Foundation, AdRoll, BrightRoll, Rackspace//Geekdom, Hack Reactor, Tagged, Chartboost, Zynga.org, DeNA and Glu Mobile. And Pocket Gems generously hosted Mission Bit's Demo Day.

    Kiba

    Monty Choy, Kelly Huang, Herman Lin, Chris Lim

    Story: Kiba is one of the wolves in his wolf pack, and he was bullied by other members of the pack. He must go through the trial of wolves in order to strengthen his ability and prove himself to his comrades. He must take the trial to go from pup to a mature, powerful wolf. There are monstrous creatures on his road of trial. In the end, he proves himself worthy and becomes the very best like no one ever was.
    Mechanics: Hold the left side of the screen to move back. Hold the right side of the screen to move forward. Tap the enemy to automatically move to a reasonable range, and attack the enemy once. Swipe left to go back swiftly (in order to dodge enemy attack). Swipe right to dash forward (needs upgrade. Tap the player itself to activate defense (reduce damage). Open upgrade menu by pressing menu located on the upper right corner, which enables player to upgrade the character’s skills
    http://www.missionbit.com/projects/kiba

    Snail Assalt

    Douglas Mejia, Milo Kahney, Vivian Lam, Ryan Shee, Lin Chen

    Story: Jimmy is this asshole kid who picks on snails and ants and whatnot. While his parents are out on vacation. King Snaily (mutated satan offspring snail, with human intelligence and a bad temper) wants to conquer world. So Snailey begins his evil plan at Jimmy’s house first. Jimmy must stop snaily and his hordes and protect his house. Tried to warn people of Snailey’s plan, no one believed him. Called him crazy.
    Mechanics: Tap/hold to shoot. (Water has recharge bar thing, pump watergun to refill) Double tap for extra power (use up more water) if house destroyed level lost, snaily wins fortify house and upgrade weapons acquire gold/snail shell to buy upgrades vine to protect house… temp barrier 4 lanes where snails attack from.
    http://www.missionbit.com/projects/snail-assalt

    Protaters

    Ryan Hu, Tommy Wong, Susan Tham

    Story: You’re a magician whose sole duty is to protect your village from hordes and hordes of monsters who are trying to steal your village’s taters(potatoes) in other to make tater tots. As the magician you can never “die” however monsters can attack you and if they bring your HP down to 0 you “die”, but are later revived at your village, well and healthy. However if the village is destroyed, then the game is lost. There are about 16 levels you can play, or you can play the endless Sandbox mode to challenge yourself to an infinite horde. As you beat levels you have the chance to upgrade your magician and/or village to help you and your village to last against the horde, using your spoils of war (gold) and points which can be converted to your magician’s EXP or your magician’s skills.
    Mechanics: Waves of monsters will spawn during a level, depending on how far you have progressed monsters will either get tougher, increase in number, or both. You must defend your village from the monsters using the magician you control. As the magician you will have a basic attack, skills, and items. Attacks and skills can be upgraded, while items can be bought or picked up as monster drops. You can move left or right to either fall down to the village or engage any enemies. Your village has HP, if that HP reaches zero, you lose. Monsters will gradually bring down your village’s HP when they reach your village. If your village is still standing at the end of the level you win that level and gain points and gold from the level, which is spent on items and/or upgrades
    http://www.missionbit.com/projects/protaters
