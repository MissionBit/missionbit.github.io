Course Overview and Objectives

Course Description

This course is specifically designed for students with little to no coding experience. Students will learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as some core programming concepts through project based instruction. Throughout the semester, students will solve mini coding challenges and work on 3 mini projects: an adventure game, a digital poem, and a portfolio. For their final project, the students will design and build their own website based on their interests.

Topics include: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and UX Design.

Learning Goals

  • Technical skills:
    • Design, implement, and publish a website
    • Describe the main features of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Design Thinking techniques: Brainstorming, Storyboarding, and Prototyping.
  • Other goals:
    • Students will work effectively on teams and review each other's code.
    • Students will feel like they belong in tech
    • Students will have insights into what itâ€™s like to work in the tech industry (pros and cons)
    • Students will be able to pitch their project idea
    • (Optional) Students will develop public speaking skills
  • Mini Projects:
    • Adventure Game
    • Digital Poetry
    • Portfolio
  • Final Project: Website - Open theme
