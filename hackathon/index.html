Hack-A-Bit

Join Us for a Day of Hacking

We provide the space for you and computers to work on. All you have to do is bring your creativity and imagination. You will work with a team to develop your project and show off your skills at with a presentation. Prizes will be awarded to the top teams.

Volunteers
