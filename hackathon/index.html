Join us for a day of hacking!

At Mission Bit, we’re working to close the tech divide through a three-part strategy of exposing, engaging and employing young people to pursue college degrees in computer science and careers as software engineers.


What would happen if students designed their own learning platform?

As we expand and consider building a custom learning management system (LMS), we want to put students at the forefront of this growth and give them an opportunity to design and prototype our LMS. This event will include three workshops, and at the end of the day each team will be able to pitch their idea and compete for prizes.

We believe this hackathon will encourage students to actively participate in designing their future.
